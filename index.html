🏛️ About Madarsa Haveri

Madarsa Haveri is a premier Islamic educational institution dedicated to providing quality religious education along with modern subjects. Established in 1990, we have been serving the community for over three decades.

🎯 Our Mission

To nurture well-rounded Muslim individuals who are grounded in Islamic knowledge while being equipped with modern skills to contribute positively to society.

👁️ Our Vision

To become a leading institution in Islamic education that produces scholars who can address contemporary challenges with Islamic wisdom.

🏆 Achievements

  • ✅ 500+ Students Graduated
  • ✅ 50+ Hafiz-e-Quran Produced
  • ✅ State Level Academic Excellence Award
  • ✅ Community Service Recognition
  • ✅ Inter-Madarsa Competition Winners

📚 Our Courses

📖 Quran Memorization (Hifz)

Duration: 3-5 years

Age Group: 7-15 years

Complete memorization of the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed and understanding.

📜 Islamic Studies (Aalim Course)

Duration: 8 years

Age Group: 14+ years

Comprehensive study of Islamic jurisprudence, theology, history, and Arabic language.

🕌 Basic Islamic Education

Duration: 3 years

Age Group: 5-10 years

Fundamental Islamic knowledge including Quran reading, basic prayers, and Islamic manners.

💻 Modern Education

Subjects: English, Mathematics, Science, Computer

Alongside Islamic education, we provide modern subjects to ensure holistic development.
